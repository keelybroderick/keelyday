Executive Summary

Art Direction | Graphic Design | UX

The Old Town Commercial Association is a non-profit organization revitalizing Lansing Michigan's Old Town. However, they have faced challenges in maintaining an active presence on social media and establishing a consistent brand identity. To address these issues, MSU Street Team Charcoal collaborated with Old Town Commercial Association to create a brand book and implement it through various community engagement activities, including an interactive bucket list and a video series highlighting the various local businesses.

Problem

The Old Town Commercial Association lacked consistency in their brand identity and presence, which hindered their efforts to effectively promote the revitalization of Lansing's Old Town. This resulted in limited community engagement and awareness of the organization's mission.

Solution

MSU Street Teams collaborated with the Old Town Commercial Association to create a brand book that established a consistent brand identity for the organization. This brand book served as a guide for all communication materials. By implementing a cohesive brand identity, the Old Town Commercial Association was able to improve their community engagement efforts and increase awareness of their initiatives.

Opportunity

Establish a strong brand presence and effectively promote their businesses. With a consistent brand identity, the organization can leverage social media, interactive activities, and other marketing materials to engage with the community, highlight local businesses, and position Old Town as a fun and eclectic destination. This will contribute to the overall success of the revitalization efforts and help the Old Town Commercial Association achieve their mission.
