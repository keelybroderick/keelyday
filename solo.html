Executive Summary

Art Direction | Graphic Design | Production | UX

ElectraMeccanica's SOLO is a groundbreaking, single-seat, fully electric commuter vehicle. The company conducted a comprehensive analysis of the branding of electric vehicles (EVs) and their Instagram presence, and identified an untapped corner in the market. By positioning the SOLO as a financially accessible vehicle that fits seamlessly into the user's lifestyle, offering unique features such as trunk space, dual entry points, ease of parking, and a cockpit-like experience, the campaign aimed to drive engagement and sales. This was achieved through the design of a new website, commercials, and content creation during a 4-day still and video production shoot at various locations in Beverly Hills, Malibu, Melrose, and El Segundo, with behind-the-scenes footage captured for the client.

Problem

Establish the SOLO as a unique and financially accessible option in the competitive market of EVs. The branding and Instagram presence of EVs had been extensively explored, and the campaign identified a unique positioning for the SOLO that would resonate with potential buyers and differentiate it from other EVs.

Solution

Positioned the SOLO as a practical and lifestyle-oriented vehicle for daily commutes, highlighting its unique features and benefits such as trunk space, dual entry points, ease of parking, and a cockpit-like experience. The campaign included the design of a new website, commercials, and content creation during a 4-day production shoot at premium locations in Los Angeles, creating engaging and visually appealing content for the website and social media channels. Behind-the-scenes footage added an extra layer of engagement and transparency for the client.

Opportunity

The campaign presents an opportunity to establish the SOLO as a game-changing EV option in the market, with a unique positioning that appeals to potential buyers seeking a practical, financially accessible, and lifestyle-oriented vehicle for their daily activities. The visually appealing content created during the production shoot, along with the new website and commercials, can drive increased engagement, generate leads, and ultimately boost sales.
